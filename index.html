Dad's Nacho Dip:

Jump to recipe

   Recipe:

Ingredients:

  •    Cream Cheese
    •    
  •    Salsa
    •    
  •    Tomato- optional
    •    
  •    Grated Cheddar Cheese
    •    
  •    Chopped Green Onion- optional
    •    
  •    Sliced Jalapeño Peppers- optional
    •    
  •    9 inch dish

Steps:

       
  1.    Spread cream cheese on the bottom of dish
    2.    
  2.    Pour salsa over the cream cheese
    3.    
  3.    Spread cheese on top of the salsa
    4.    
  4.    Sprinkle on the green onion and jalapeño pepers

The Nacho Dip is now ready to be served as a snack or an appetizer with some tortilla chips!
